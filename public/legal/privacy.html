Who we are

ABN operates a community business directory and job board. We are a technology platform for discovery — not a bank, peer-payment processor, or nonprofit charity unless separately stated by a licensed entity.

Information we collect

Account data (name, email, phone, password hash), listing and job content, favorites, reports, diagnostics, and membership status for directory visibility subscriptions billed by Apple or Google.

Payments

Digital memberships are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. ABN does not collect card numbers inside the app for those memberships. Customer–business communication happens outside the app.

Your rights

Update your profile, contact support, or delete your account in the app (Account → Delete Account).
